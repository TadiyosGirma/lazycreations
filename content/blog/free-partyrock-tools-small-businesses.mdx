PartyRock makes it easy to prototype AI apps: summarize documents, compare options, draft replies. Small teams use it to remove admin friction.

Stories include busy volunteers coordinating fosters, students building study aids, and SMBs assembling planning tools.

Source: [About Amazon — PartyRock round‑up](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/aws/free-generative-ai-tool-amazon-aws-partyrock#:~:text=1,to%20guides)

SMB takeaway: list 3 repetitive tasks and build a PartyRock helper for each. Keep data sensitivity in mind.
