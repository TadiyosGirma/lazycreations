Safety documentation is essential but time‑consuming. A contractor used Amazon PartyRock to assemble site report drafts from notes and photos, then standardized checklists for common tasks.

Supervisors reviewed and finalized reports in minutes rather than hours, improving compliance and freeing time for on‑site work.

Source: [About Amazon — PartyRock stories and reports](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/aws/free-generative-ai-tool-amazon-aws-partyrock#:~:text=3,and%20reports)

SMB takeaway: start with a single report type. Automate the first draft, then refine.
