**TL;DR**

- AI adoption is now a majority phenomenon across all 50 states
- Reported benefits: faster content cycles, better targeting, and time savings
- Start small; focus on measurable outcomes and data hygiene

## Key findings

- Surveyed SMBs across the U.S. report rising AI usage in marketing, customer service, and analytics.
- Practical wins include faster iteration and improved lead handling.

## Examples and anecdotes

- Small retailers and services firms use AI for copy, recommendations, and support triage.

## Guidance for SMBs

- Identify 1–2 workflows with measurable outcomes (reply speed, meetings, AOV).
- Pilot tools, document prompts, and track a simple KPI stack.
