**TL;DR**

- Measurement frameworks are key for understanding adoption and impact
- Indicators include usage intensity, task coverage, and outcomes
- SMBs should tie metrics to business KPIs

## Key points

- The note surveys approaches to measuring AI uptake and their implications.

## Guidance for SMBs

- Track outcome metrics (response time, conversion, error rate) alongside usage to validate ROI.
