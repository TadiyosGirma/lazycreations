Agencies benefit from content curation at scale. Agency Pure leveraged AI to tailor newsletters to individual interests, improving relevance and performance.

The system selected articles based on engagement signals and topics, drafting personalized intros that editors reviewed before sending.

Source: [Marketing AI Institute — SMB marketing case studies](https://www.marketingaiinstitute.com/blog/3-case-studies-of-smbs-using-ai-for-marketing)

SMB takeaway: start with a single segment and a weekly cadence. Track open rate and click‑through versus your baseline.
