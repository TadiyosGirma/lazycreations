**TL;DR**

- Majority of SMBs report adopting AI/automation in at least one workflow
- Gains: faster cycles, better targeting, improved response times, and cost savings
- Start with a single workflow and measure outcomes against a baseline

## Key findings

- The report synthesizes national survey data on technology and AI usage by small businesses, linking adoption with performance and resilience.
- Teams report time savings, more consistent output, and improved customer experiences.

## Examples and anecdotes

- Case snapshots include marketing content acceleration, CRM enrichment, and analytics-driven decision making.

## Guidance for SMBs

- Choose one use case (content, support, or reporting). Document prompts and SOPs, and track lead response time, qualified meetings, and retention.
