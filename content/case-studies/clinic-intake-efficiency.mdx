## Problem

Paper intake and duplicate entry caused delays and errors.

## Constraints

HIPAA compliance, legacy EHR, limited staff capacity.

## Solution

Accessible web forms, validation, and secure EHR sync. Status dashboards for staff.

## Impact

• 40% admin time saved
• 18% no-shows reduced via reminders
• Errors down 70%

## Stack

Next.js, serverless functions, EHR API, Postgres, Resend, Vercel.
